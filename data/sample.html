This message indicates that there is an issue with the DNS configuration for your domain. A DNS resource record defines specific settings for your domain, such as pointing it to a web server or configuring email delivery. The error suggests that one of these records is either invalid or conflicts with another record.

\n

For example, a CNAME record cannot coexist with other record types like TXT, MX, A, or AAAA for the same hostname. If two records share the same hostname, it creates a conflict, preventing proper functionality. To resolve this, you would need to identify the conflicting records and either delete or modify one of them to ensure compatibility.

\n

On the page you're viewing, you can manage DNS records for your domain, such as CNAME, TXT, MX, and others. If you're unsure about which records to delete or modify, consulting your hosting provider for guidance is recommended. They can help you safely manage your DNS settings.
